About AshGen

AshGen is a materials technology company focused on transforming coal fly ash — one of the world's most abundant industrial byproducts — into high-value strategic resources

Our AI-enabled process produces critical metals, geopolymers, and zeolites from fly ash streams with greater efficiency and lower carbon output than conventional methods. The result is a cleaner environment, a stronger domestic supply chain, and new economic value for industries looking to decarbonize

Our Mission

We believe industrial waste should be a resource, not a liability. AshGen exists to prove that environmental responsibility and economic value creation are not in conflict — they are the same goal

What We Produce

Our low-carbon process unlocks the hidden value in fly ash, converting a widespread industrial waste stream into high-demand strategic materials

⚙️ Critical Metals

Recover rare earth and critical metals from fly ash, building a domestic supply chain independent of foreign sources

🧱 Geopolymers

Produce low-carbon geopolymer binders that can replace traditional cement, reducing CO₂ emissions in construction

🔬 Zeolites

Generate high-value zeolites used in filtration, catalysts, and environmental remediation applications
