About AshGen

AshGen is a materials technology company focused on transforming coal fly ash — one of the world's most abundant industrial byproducts — into high-value strategic resources.

Our proprietary AI-enabled process identifies and extracts critical metals, geopolymers, and zeolites from fly ash streams with greater efficiency and lower carbon output than conventional methods. The result is a cleaner environment, a stronger domestic supply chain, and new economic value for industries looking to decarbonize.

Our Mission

We believe industrial waste should be a resource, not a liability. AshGen exists to prove that environmental responsibility and economic value creation are not in conflict — they are the same goal.

Our Impact

🌍 Decarbonization

Our low-carbon process helps cement, mining, and energy companies reduce their environmental footprint without sacrificing productivity.

🇺🇸 Domestic Supply

By recovering critical metals from fly ash, AshGen helps reduce U.S. dependence on foreign sources for materials essential to manufacturing and clean energy.

♻️ Waste Reduction

We convert a problematic waste stream into productive inputs, reducing the land use, leaching risk, and long-term liability of fly ash storage.
