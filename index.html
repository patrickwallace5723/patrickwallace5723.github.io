Turning an Environmental Liability into Strategic Value

AshGen uses AI-enabled technology to transform coal fly ash into critical metals, geopolymers, and zeolites — driving decarbonization across heavy industry while creating a domestic supply of essential resources.

What We Produce

Our low-carbon process unlocks the hidden value in fly ash, converting a widespread industrial waste stream into high-demand strategic materials.

⚙️ Critical Metals

Recover rare earth and critical metals from fly ash, building a domestic supply chain independent of foreign sources.

🧱 Geopolymers

Produce low-carbon geopolymer binders that can replace traditional cement, reducing CO₂ emissions in construction.

🔬 Zeolites

Generate high-value zeolites used in filtration, catalysis, and environmental remediation applications.

Why AshGen

Fly ash is one of the largest industrial waste streams in the world. AshGen's AI-enabled design turns this challenge into an opportunity — reducing environmental liability for power producers while supplying critical materials to the industries that need them most.
